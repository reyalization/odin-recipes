Classic Waffles


Odin Recipes

A lovely, crispy waffle perfect for the morning

These classic waffles are easy to make and a very filling way to prepare a perfect breakfast

Top it off with some orange juice and you have yourself a morning to remember with your family or loved ones

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all purpose-flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1.5 cups warm milk
  • 0.3 cup butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Step 1

    (a) In a large bowl, mix together flour,salt, baking powder, and sugar; set aside
    (b) Preheat waffle iron to desired temperature

  2. Step 2

    (a) In a separate bowl, beat the eggs
    (b) Stir in the milk, butter, and vanilla
    (c) Pour the milk mixture into the flour mixture; beat until blended

  3. Step 3

    (a) Ladle the batter into a preheated waffle iron
    (b) Cook the waffles until golden and crisp
    (c) Serve immediately
