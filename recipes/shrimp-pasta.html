Garlic Shrimp Pasta Bake


Odin Recipes

Garlick Shrimp Pasta goodness to die for

Want a super satisfying, quick, and easy dinner?

Then this buttery garlic shrimp pasta bake is definetely a family pleasing meal idea that you should try!

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon vegetable oil
  • 1 (10 ounce) package penne pasta
  • 3 tablespoons butter, divided
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 pound uncooked medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 2 teaspoons chopped fresh dill
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 large tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • ½ cup grated Romano cheese
  • ¼ cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Step 1

    (a) Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

  2. Step 2

    (a) Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
    (b) Add penne and cook, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 11 minutes.
    (c) Drain.

  3. Step 3

    (a) Heat 1 tablespoon butter and vegetable oil large oven-safe skillet over medium heat.
    (b) Add garlic; cook until fragrant, about 30 seconds.
    (c) Add shrimp, 2 tablespoons parsley, and dill.
    (d) Cook and stir, flipping shrimp halfway, until pink, 3 to 4 minutes.
    (e) Move shrimp to a plate and set aside.

  4. Step 4

    (a) Melt the remaining 2 tablespoons butter in the skillet, stirring into the shrimp juices.
    (b) Add flour; whisk until fully combined and starting to turn golden, 30 seconds to 1 minute.
    (c) Add chicken broth and milk in that order; whisk to combine.
    (d) Stir in tomatoes, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
    (e) Bring sauce to a simmer.

  5. Step 5

    (a) Stir 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese and Romano cheese into the sauce until cheeses begin to melt and sauce is creamy.
    (b) Add 1 tablespoon mozzarella cheese.
    (c) Mix in about 1/2 the cooked penne to start; add more if there is enough sauce to coat.
    (d) Add the shrimp and stir to combine.
    (e) Top with the remaining parsley, Parmesan, and mozzarella.

  6. Step 6

    (a) Bake in the preheated oven and bake until top is golden and sauce bubbles around the edges of the skillet, 8 to 10 minutes.
