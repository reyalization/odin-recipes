Miracle Lasagna


Odin Recipes

Traditional, full-flavored lasagna is layered together in just minutes with pasta sauce, noodles, and three cheeses.

This lasagna is an easy but flavorful recipe which is sure to impress your guests

Top it off with some wine and you have yourself an evening to remember with your family or loved ones

Ingredients

  • Prego Traditional Italian Sauce
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Step 1

    (a) Spread about 1 cup pasta sauce in 2-quart shallow baking dish (11x7-inch).
    (b) Top with 3 uncooked noodles, ricotta cheese, 1 cup mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese and 1 cup pasta sauce.
    (c) Top with remaining 3 uncooked noodles and remaining pasta sauce. Cover.

  2. Step 2

    (a) Bake at 375 degrees F for 1 hour
    (b) Top with remaining mozzarella cheese
    (c) Let stand for 5 minutes
